Analysts

INTJ – Architect

Strategic and logical minds, natural planners.

INTJs are strategic visionaries who love to devise innovative solutions to complex problems. Often seen as independent and self-confident, they are natural long-term planners.

  • Prefer structure and efficiency in all areas of life
  • Enjoy intellectual challenges and strategic games
  • Can be private and selective with social relationships
  • Thrive in roles that require autonomy and deep thinking

INTP – Logician

Innovative and curious thinkers, always exploring ideas.

INTPs are driven by a thirst for understanding and knowledge. They're independent thinkers who love dissecting theories and finding underlying principles.

  • Highly analytical and abstract in their thinking
  • Often get lost in their own thoughts and ideas
  • Value truth and logic over social conventions
  • Struggle with structure but excel in freeform environments

ENTJ – Commander

Confident leaders who excel at setting and achieving goals.

ENTJs are bold, imaginative leaders who see inefficiency and immediately seek ways to fix it. Their confidence and charisma make them natural leaders in any setting.

  • Excellent at organizing people and systems
  • Driven by goals, ambition, and logical planning
  • May come off as overly blunt or assertive
  • Thrive in high-pressure environments

ENTP – Debater

Energetic and curious, love engaging in intellectual battles.

ENTPs are energetic, quick-witted individuals who thrive on new ideas and discussions. They enjoy challenging norms and pushing boundaries.

  • Loves to brainstorm and experiment with ideas
  • Often plays devil’s advocate in debates
  • Quick thinkers, often excelling in improvisation
  • Can struggle with follow-through or routine tasks

Diplomats

INFJ – Advocate

Quiet, idealistic and passionate about causes.

INFJs are deeply insightful and compassionate individuals who strive to make a difference. They combine vision with empathy in meaningful ways.

  • Value authenticity and integrity
  • Have strong intuitive understanding of others
  • Prefer deep, meaningful conversations
  • Often excel in creative or counseling roles

INFP – Mediator

Poetic, kind and altruistic individuals, always eager to help.

INFPs are imaginative idealists driven by strong values and a desire to make the world better. They seek personal growth and connection.

  • Deeply empathetic and understanding
  • Enjoy writing, art, and other expressive outlets
  • Often have a strong sense of purpose
  • Can appear reserved but have rich inner lives

ENFJ – Protagonist

Charismatic and inspiring leaders, able to mesmerize their audience.

ENFJs are natural-born leaders and deeply care about others. They work to bring people together and motivate them toward shared goals.

  • Highly empathetic and driven by service
  • Excel at public speaking and team building
  • Often take on mentoring or guiding roles
  • Value harmony and meaningful relationships

ENFP – Campaigner

Enthusiastic, creative, and sociable free spirits.

ENFPs are passionate individuals full of energy and imagination. They thrive on connections and possibilities for the future.

  • Loves exploring new ideas and places
  • Warm and emotionally expressive
  • Values authenticity and personal growth
  • Dislikes routine and restrictions

Sentinels

ISTJ – Logistician

Practical and fact-minded individuals, reliable and dutiful.

ISTJs are serious, responsible, and dependable. They take obligations seriously and value tradition and honesty.

  • Excel at organization and task completion
  • Prefer established procedures and routines
  • Highly detail-oriented and efficient
  • Uphold rules and duty above all

ISFJ – Defender

Protective and warm, always ready to defend loved ones.

ISFJs are gentle, devoted, and detail-oriented. They quietly protect and care for others, often working behind the scenes.

  • Compassionate and deeply loyal
  • Thrive in structured and predictable roles
  • Remember small details about people they care about
  • Dislike confrontation but will stand up for others

ESTJ – Executive

Excellent administrators, unsurpassed at managing things or people.

ESTJs are practical organizers and natural leaders. They take charge of projects and ensure they are completed efficiently and on time.

  • Strong-willed and confident decision makers
  • Value tradition, order, and rules
  • Excel in leadership and managerial roles
  • Respect hard work and responsibility

ESFJ – Consul

Extraordinarily caring, social and popular people, always eager to help.

ESFJs are warm, nurturing individuals who thrive in structured social settings. They put others’ needs before their own and seek community harmony.

  • Highly aware of others’ emotions and needs
  • Enjoy traditions, celebrations, and group activities
  • Often take on caretaker or social organizer roles
  • Can struggle with criticism or disharmony

Explorers

ISTP – Virtuoso

Bold and practical experimenters, masters of tools.

ISTPs are hands-on problem solvers who love figuring out how things work. They’re independent and action-oriented.

  • Prefer doing over theorizing
  • Enjoy building, fixing, and experimenting
  • Dislike being controlled or micromanaged
  • Cool-headed and spontaneous under pressure

ISFP – Adventurer

Flexible and charming artists, always ready to explore.

ISFPs are gentle, spontaneous, and deeply artistic. They appreciate beauty and often express themselves through action and creativity.

  • Highly sensitive to aesthetics and sensory experiences
  • Prefer freedom and exploration to strict routines
  • Often shy but fiercely loyal to their values
  • Seek harmony and peace in relationships

ESTP – Entrepreneur

Smart, energetic and perceptive individuals, who love to live on the edge.

ESTPs are energetic, outgoing risk-takers who enjoy fast-paced environments. They act quickly and are confident in social settings.

  • Loves action and physical challenges
  • Charismatic and persuasive
  • Excels in improvisation and thinking on their feet
  • Can struggle with long-term planning

ESFP – Entertainer

Spontaneous, energetic and enthusiastic entertainers.

ESFPs live in the moment and spread joy wherever they go. They love excitement, attention, and new experiences.

  • Natural performers and entertainers
  • Highly social and expressive
  • Find routine dull and limiting
  • Motivated by fun, connection, and experience
